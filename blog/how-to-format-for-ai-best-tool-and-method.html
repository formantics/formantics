How to Format for AI: Best Tool and Method

The best way to format for AI is to turn your existing formatting (bold, colors, highlights) into explicit structure AI can read. Here’s the method and the best free format-for-AI tool.

Why You Need to Format for AI

When you paste content into ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI tools, formatting is stripped. Bold, colors, and highlights disappear — and with them, the meaning you intended. To get accurate summaries and responses, you need to format for AI: give the model clear structure (e.g. “Question:” and “Answer:”) instead of relying on visual cues it never sees.

The Best Method to Format Text for AI

Format text for AI in three steps:

  • Step 1: Paste your formatted content (from Word, email, Docs, or PDF) into a format-for-AI tool.
  • Step 2: Assign a meaning to each format — e.g. bold = Question, red = Answer, yellow highlight = Key point.
  • Step 3: Copy the tagged output and paste it into ChatGPT or your AI tool. The structure is now in the text, so the AI can use it.

This method works for any document: format document for AI by mapping your visual formatting to semantic tags. No manual rewriting.
